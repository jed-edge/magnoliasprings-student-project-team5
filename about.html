Why Visit the Festival?

  • Picturesque Downtown: Experience a charming historic town square adorned with vibrant fall decorations, colorful foliage, pumpkins, and seasonal displays.
  • Live Entertainment: Enjoy live music, performances, and entertainment from talented local and regional artists throughout the day.
  • Artisan Marketplace: Browse a curated selection of handcrafted goods, artwork, specialty items, and unique gifts from local artisans and vendors.
  • Food & Seasonal Treats: Savor a wide variety of festival favorites, Southern cuisine, fresh baked goods, and classic autumn treats from local food vendors.
  • Family-Friendly Activities: Participate in games, children's attractions, pumpkin-themed activities, and interactive experiences designed for visitors of all ages.
  • Community Showcase: Meet local organizations, civic groups, and nonprofits highlighting programs, services, and opportunities to get involved.
  • A Memorable Tradition: Celebrate the beauty of autumn while creating lasting memories with family and friends in a warm, welcoming Southern community.

History

The Magnolia Springs Fall Festival began as a small community celebration of autumn and our historic downtown. Over the years, it has grown into one of the area's favorite annual traditions, featuring local vendors, live entertainment, family activities, and seasonal attractions. Today, the festival continues to celebrate community spirit, support local businesses, and bring residents and visitors together to enjoy the best of the fall season.
