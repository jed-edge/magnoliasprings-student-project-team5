Overview of Magnolia Springs 2024 Fall Festival

Welcome to the official website of the Magnolia Springs 2024 Fall Festival! This beloved annual event celebrates the beauty of fall in our charming Southern town. Held on the second Friday and Saturday in October, the festival transforms our historic town square into a vibrant celebration filled with live music, delicious food trucks, unique arts and crafts vendors, and a bustling farmers' market. Whether you're a local or visiting from out of town, the Magnolia Springs Fall Festival offers something for everyone.

Key Event Highlights

Enjoy live performances, shop for handmade treasures, taste seasonal treats, and create lasting memories with family and friends. Come experience the warm Southern hospitality and festive atmosphere that make this event a highlight of the fall season!
